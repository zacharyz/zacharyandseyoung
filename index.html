The Wedding

Seyoung and Zachary are getting married on September 14, 2013, after seven years of being a couple. They met and began dating in Eugene, Oregon and have continued to build their life together in Portland, Oregon with their beloved cat Bella. The couple enjoy enriching their relationship with shared experiences like traveling, exploring local food and entertainment offered in Portland, and exchanging silly inside jokes.

There is a Korean folktale about a poor but kind man who saved a barn swallow's life. The barn swallow repaid his kindness with a gourd seed. The man planted the seed and grew several giant gourds. When the man and his family cut them open, the gourds were filled with riches that helped the family live comfortably and happily for the rest of their days.

We have adopted the value of kindness begetting happiness to become the theme for our wedding. In the years that we have been together many have shown support for the solidity of their relationship. The theme of this wedding is dedicated to these people, whose support and kindness have helped us to pursue our happiness, as the barn swallows did in the story.

We would like to invite you to celebrate with us the seven years passed and the many years that will follow, and allow us to thank you for your support throughout the years.

Directions

The Wedding and Reception will be held at the Historic Overlook House in north Portland.

Northbound from Interstate 5:

  • Take Exit 303 (Swan Island) Stay left to Interstate Ave.
  • Turn left and proceed 3/10 miles on Interstate Ave.
  • Turn right on N. Overlook Blvd.
  • Proceed two blocks.
  • Veer Left onto Melrose Drive (one way) and proceed 2/10 mile.
  • The Overlook House is located on the left

Southbound from Interstate 5:

  • Take Exit 303 (Alberta Street).
  • At first stop sign (Alberta Street) turn right.
  • At first light (Interstate Avenue) turn left and proceed 1/2 miles.
  • Turn right on N. Overlook Blvd.
  • Proceed two blocks.
  • Veer Left onto Melrose Drive (one way) proceed 2/10 mile.
  • The Overlook House is located on the left.

TriMet's Max Yellow Line (Portland's Light Rail System):

The Interstate Max/Yellow line "Overlook Park" Station is three blocks from the Overlook House. From the station (at the corner of Interstate Ave and Overlook Blvd) walk west on Overlook Blvd., along the park proceeding left for 2/10 of a mile on Melrose Drive (one way) Overlook House is located on the left.
